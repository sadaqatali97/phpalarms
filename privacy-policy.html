A cookie is a small file containing a string of characters that is sent to your web browser when you visit a website. When you visit the website again from that web browser, the cookie allows that site to recognize your browser and, potentially, device. Cookies may store unique identifiers, user preferences and other Personal Information. You can direct your browser to clear all cookies or use the browser to understand which cookies are being set and what network requests are being made by our site.
